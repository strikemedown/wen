Valentines day:

A day on Feburary 14th every year. A day to celebrate your love and friendships. Write letters, exchange gifts, go out for dinner, spend time together or just spend time together. This could be with friends, family or a date. And now a poem:

Letters that were never sent

Stay,

If that's' what you expect me to say, you're wrong

I can't say that,

I loved you.

And to be honest,

I hated you

I would be lying to myself if I said,

I want to talk to you.

Why is everything so different now?

You might have an answer to share.

But before you do,

Leave.

Now read it up from bottom to top.

-Jason R. M.
