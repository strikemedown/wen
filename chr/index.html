Christmas

Fun Facts about Christmas:

  • It takes 15 years to grow a Christmas tree
  • The first song played in space was Jingle Bells
  • The tallest tree ever displayed was in Seattle at 221 feet tall
  • The candy cane was invented in Germany and shaped like a J for Jesus.
  • The longest running Santa Claus parade is in Illinois
  • The Grinch is the highest grossing Christmas film at $512 million and second to that is Home Alone at $477 million.
